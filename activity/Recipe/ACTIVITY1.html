Omelet Roll

    Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk (optional – makes omelet fluffier)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter

    Recipe

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl, whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until smooth
  2. Add oil/butter to a non-stick pan and heat on medium-low.
  3. Pour a small portion of egg (about 1/3rd) into the pan, tilting to spread evenly.
  4. When the egg is half-cooked (still slightly soft on top), start rolling it gently from one side with a spatula. Push the rolled part to one side of the pan.
  5. Pour another thin layer of egg into the empty space of the pan. Let it cook, then roll again, wrapping it around the first roll.
  6. Repeat until all egg is used.
  7. Take out the omelet roll, let it rest for 1–2 minutes, then slice into thick pieces.
